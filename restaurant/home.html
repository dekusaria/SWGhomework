The Blackened Crust was founded in July of 2020 by two aged industry veterans, eager to showcase their own spin on pizza. Minnesota locals from all walks of life have helped to establish this new restaurant as a place where everyone can come together and enjoy a delicious meal. With locally sourced ingredients, uniquely inspired recipes, and selection of beer from MN breweries, The Blackened Crust provides a dining experience only Minnesotans could bring to the table.
